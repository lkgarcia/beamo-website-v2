FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of workspaces does Beamo offer?

We offer a range of workspace solutions including private offices, dedicated desks, hot desks, and meeting rooms. All come with high-speed internet, 24/7 access, and complimentary amenities like our coffee bar and lounge areas.

Can I use both workspace and digital services?

Absolutely! Many of our clients take advantage of both offerings. We provide bundled packages that combine workspace access with digital services at a discounted rate, giving you everything you need under one roof.

How long are workspace contracts?

We pride ourselves on flexibility. Workspace memberships start from daily passes and extend to monthly or annual agreements. There are no long-term lock-ins — scale up or down as your business needs change.

What digital services do you specialise in?

Our digital team covers web development, mobile app development, UI/UX design, business automation, cloud solutions, digital marketing, data analytics, and strategic IT consulting. We work with businesses of all sizes.

How do I get started with Beamo?

Simply reach out through our contact form or click "Get Started" to schedule a tour or consultation. Our team will work with you to find the perfect workspace plan or digital solution for your needs.
