Product

DeskHubPilot

Smart Workstation Management & Usage Billing Platform

Take Full Control of Shared Workstations

DeskHubPilot is a centralized workstation management platform designed for environments where multiple users share computers. Monitor usage, automate time-based billing, enforce security policies, and remotely control machines — all from a single dashboard.

Get Started Visit deskhubpilot.com
The Problem

What Problem It Solves

If you operate shared computer environments, you likely face challenges that DeskHubPilot is built to eliminate.

Manual Monitoring

Eliminate the need for staff to manually track workstation usage. DeskHubPilot gives you real-time visibility from a single dashboard.

Inaccurate Time Billing

Replace error-prone manual timekeeping with automated, precise usage tracking and billing that logs every session accurately.

Idle Machines Left Unlocked

Automatically lock workstations after idle periods, preventing unauthorized access and protecting sensitive data.

Security Risks

Block USB ports, restrict unauthorized downloads, and enforce access policies to protect your environment and data.

No Multi-Site Visibility

Manage and monitor workstations across multiple branches and locations from a single centralized dashboard.

Process

How It Works

Secure. Centralized. Scalable. — DeskHubPilot connects every workstation to a single intelligent platform.

1

Lightweight Agent Installed

A lightweight software agent is installed on each workstation, enabling real-time communication with the central management server.

2

Machines Connect to Central Server

All workstations connect to the central management server, creating a unified, visible network of devices.

3

Admin Dashboard Provides Real-Time Visibility

The admin dashboard gives you a live view of all machines — active sessions, idle units, logged-in users, and machine status at a glance.

4

Usage Data Is Logged and Processed

Every session is recorded with precision — who used which machine, for how long, and at what time — building a complete usage history.

5

Reports and Billing Generated Automatically

Usage reports and billing summaries are generated automatically, giving you accurate data for invoicing and business decisions.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this work offline?

Core functionality requires network connectivity to the management server. Deployment options can be designed for resilience to handle intermittent connectivity scenarios.

Is it secure?

Yes. Policy enforcement and device control mechanisms are built into the platform. Beamo will also help you design and enforce security policies tailored to your environment and compliance requirements.

Can it support multiple locations?

Yes. Enterprise setups can manage multiple branches from a single dashboard, giving you unified visibility and control across all your sites.

Can billing be customized?

Yes. Time-based billing structures can be configured to match your pricing model — whether you charge by the hour, by session, or through a custom rate structure.
