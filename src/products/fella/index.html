Features

What Fella Does

A complete digital dining solution that connects your customers, kitchen, and management — all through a simple QR code.

QR-Based Menu Access

Customers scan a QR code at the table to instantly access the full digital menu on their own device — no app download required.

Interactive Digital Menu

Rich menu with photos, descriptions, allergen info, and categories. Customers can browse at their own pace with an intuitive interface.

Direct Ordering from Device

Customers place orders directly from their phone or tablet — reducing wait times and eliminating the need for staff to take orders manually.

Real-Time Kitchen Transmission

Orders are sent directly to the kitchen display or POS system the moment they're placed, eliminating manual order relays and reducing errors.

Centralised Admin Dashboard

Manage all your menus, orders, and analytics from a single web dashboard — accessible from any device, anywhere.

Easy Menu Updates

Update prices, add new dishes, or remove items instantly through the dashboard — changes go live immediately with no reprinting needed.

Process

How It Works

From table to kitchen in seconds — Fella makes every step effortless.

1

Customer Scans the QR Code

A unique QR code at each table or counter takes customers directly to the digital menu — no app, no account, no friction.

2

Menu Opens on Their Device

The full menu loads instantly in their browser — complete with photos, item descriptions, prices, and any current specials.

3

Customer Selects and Orders

Customers browse, choose their items, customise as needed, and submit their order — all from their own phone.

4

Order Sent to Kitchen or POS

The order appears instantly on the kitchen display or is pushed to your existing POS system — no manual relay needed.

5

Staff Prepares and Serves

Your team focuses on preparing and delivering great food — not taking orders. Faster throughput, fewer errors, happier customers.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do customers need to download an app to use Fella?

No app download is required. Customers simply scan the QR code with their phone's built-in camera and the menu opens directly in their browser. There's no sign-up or installation needed — it works on any modern smartphone.

Can the menu be customised with our branding?

Yes. As part of the Beamo implementation service, the customer-facing menu is fully customised with your brand colours, logo, typography, and visual style. Your guests will experience a seamless branded digital menu that reflects your identity.

Can Fella integrate with our existing POS system?

Yes. Fella supports integration with a wide range of point-of-sale systems. Our team will handle the technical integration as part of the setup process, ensuring orders flow seamlessly from the customer's device to your kitchen or counter.

Does Fella support multiple branches or locations?

Yes. Multi-branch management is available as an add-on. You can manage separate menus, pricing, and settings for each location from a single centralised admin dashboard — perfect for restaurant chains, hotel groups, or food court operators.
